## Why Advertise Through AI?

### The New Frontier of User Engagement

Users are increasingly turning to AI assistants for advice, recommendations, and decision-making. AttentionMarket puts your brand in these high-intent conversations.

**Traditional Ads vs. AI-Native Ads:**

| Traditional Display Ads | AttentionMarket AI Ads |
|------------------------|------------------------|
| Low engagement rates | Higher engagement potential |
| Interruption-based | Conversation-based |
| Cookie tracking required | Context-only matching |
| Banner blindness | Natural integration |
| Broad targeting | Intent-based precision |

### Why AI-Native Advertising Works

- **Contextual Relevance**: Ads appear when users express related intent
- **Natural Integration**: Promotions feel like helpful suggestions
- **No Tracking Required**: Privacy-first approach using only conversation context
- **Performance-Based**: Pay only for actual clicks
